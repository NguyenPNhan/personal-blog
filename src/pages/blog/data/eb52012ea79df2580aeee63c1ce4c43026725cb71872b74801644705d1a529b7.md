The best personal websites feel less like finished products and more like living notebooks. They leave room for rough ideas, useful mistakes, and connections that only become visible over time.

## One home for the work

I built this site to keep those ideas in one place. The blog is for lessons and observations, projects capture the things I make, and research holds deeper explorations that need more time.

My goal is simple: **write clearly, build thoughtfully, and return often enough to notice how the work changes.**
